About LV Beach Camp

Where relaxation meets adventure 🌊

Our Story

LV Beach Camp was born from a dream — to create a simple, peaceful, and affordable beach getaway where families, friends, and travelers can reconnect with nature. Located in Purok Santol, Brgy. Ingan, Hinunangan, Southern Leyte, we offer rustic nipa huts, cozy teepees, and open spaces where the sea breeze and sunsets do all the talking.

What started as a small local escape has grown into one of Southern Leyte’s hidden gems. From serene mornings to island-hopping adventures, LV Beach Camp continues to embrace simplicity, hospitality, and the beauty of coastal living.

Our Mission

To provide guests with an authentic island experience that blends comfort, culture, and connection to nature — offering every visitor a peaceful escape from the busy world.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a sustainable and community-driven beach camp that promotes local tourism, environmental care, and the warm hospitality of Southern Leyte.
