About LV Beach Camp

Built by the shore for travelers who love simple island life

LV Beach Camp is a cozy beachfront getaway in Hinunangan, Southern Leyte. From teepee huts and native kubos to camping tents and island hopping, we make it easy to relax, recharge, and reconnect with nature.

Mission

Create a serene, affordable beach experience where guests feel at home by the sea.

Vision

To be Southern Leyte’s favorite coastal escape — known for heartfelt hospitality and island adventures.

Why Guests Love Us

Beachfront views • Bonfire nights • Kitchen access • Island tours
